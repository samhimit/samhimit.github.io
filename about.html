Hi, I'm Samhi Mitra. I'm from St. Louis, Missouri. I have passions in Sound Design, Game Development and Music. I'm passionate about telling stories and creating unique experiences.

I'm currently studying at the University of Southern California as a Computer Science Major at the Viterbi School of Engineering. I'm also pursuing a Game Audio minor in the renowned USC Games department.

I've always known I've wanted to work in games, but my passion for sound design was sparked by the moment my instrument appeared in a Bungie ViDoc on the sound design for Destiny. I've not followed a linear path to get here, following passions in percussion and software engineering. I'm a classically trained percussionist and marched with the Colts Drum and Bugle Corps in the front ensemble.

I love reading books, running, 3d printing, and playing TTRPGs
