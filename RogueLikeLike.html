I developed RogueLikeLike in Unity with a modular card system. This was a super fun challenge that took a lot of programming chops and a lot of OOP concepts. Creating the sounds for the game was also fun incorporating 8-bit sounds with other effects to create something unique. Use WASD to move and the mouse to case spells.
